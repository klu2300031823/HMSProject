FAQs

1. What is a Health Management System?

A Health Management System is a software solution that helps in managing health records, appointments, and communication between patients and healthcare providers.

2. How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by logging into your account, selecting a doctor, and choosing a suitable date and time from the available slots.

3. Are my health records secure?

Yes, all your health records are securely stored and accessible only by you and your authorized healthcare providers.

4. How do I update my contact information?

Go to your profile settings and update your contact information. Make sure to save the changes.

5. Whom can I contact for technical support?

You can contact our support team via email at support@healthsystem.com or call us at 1800-123-456.
